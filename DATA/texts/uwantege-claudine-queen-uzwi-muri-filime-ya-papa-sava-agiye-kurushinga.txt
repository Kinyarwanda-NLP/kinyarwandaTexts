Uwantege Claudine uzwi nka Queen  muri filime z’uruhererekane za Papa Sava agiye kurushinga n’umusore bamaze igihe bakundana.
 
Uwantege ubusanzwe ukunze kwiyita Queen akaba icyamamare muri Filime ya Papa Sava yamaze gutangaza amatariki y’ubukwe bwe n’umusore witwa Baptiste ukunze kwiyita Jimmy.
 
Aba bamaze igihe bakundana bamaze gufata icyemezo cyo kurushinga ndetse bamaze gushyira hanze integuza y’ubukwe bwabo.
 
Ubukwe bwa Uwantege byitezwe ko buzaba tariki 22 Gashyantare 2020 nyuma y’iyi nteguza hakazatangwa ubutumire bwa nyabwo bw’ubukwe.
 
Uwantege si filime nyinshi yakinnyemo mu Rwanda cyane ko Papa Sava ariyo urebye yamenyekaniyemo.
 
Papa Sava ni filime y’uruhererekane inyuzwa kuri Youtube, yandikwa na Niyitegeka Gratien wamenyekanye ku mazina atandukanye nka Seburikoko, Sekaganda n’andi.
 
Iyi filime iri mu zikurikirwa n’abantu benshi mu Rwanda ndetse no hanze yarwo cyane cyane ku bakurikira Youtube.
