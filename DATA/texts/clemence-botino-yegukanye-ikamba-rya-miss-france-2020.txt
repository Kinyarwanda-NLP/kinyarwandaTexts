Mu ijoro ryo kuri uyu wa 14 Ukuboza 2019 nibwo mu Bufaransa batoraga umukobwa uhiga abandi uburanga, irushanwa ryabaga ku nshuro ya 90 ryegukanywe na Clémence Botino witabiriye iri rushanwa ahagarariye agace ka Guadeloupe.
 
Mu ijoro ryo kuri uyu wa 14 Ukuboza 2019 nibwo mu Bufaransa batoraga umukobwa uhiga abandi uburanga, irushanwa ryabaga ku nshuro ya 90 ryegukanywe na Clémence Botino witabiriye iri rushanwa ahagarariye agace ka Guadeloupe.
 
Ibi birori byabereye i Marseille byasize Vaimalama Chaves wari umaranye ikamba igihe kingana n’umwaka abonye umusimbura, ari we Clémence Botino.
 
Clémence w’imyaka 22 yabaye Nyampinga w’Ubufaransa mu gihe igisonga cye cya mbere yabaye  Lou Ruat,igisonga cya kabiri aba Matahari Bousquet, icya gatatu yabaye Sophie Diry mu gihe igisonga cya kane yabaye Manelle Souahlia.
 
Guhera tariki 11 Mata 2019 nibwo hatangajwe ko hagiye gutangira urugendo rwo gushakisha umukobwa uhiga abandi uburanga mu Bufaransa.
 
Uyu mukobwa wegukanye ikamba rya Miss France byitezwe ko azitabira amarushanwa akomeye ku Isi arimo Misss World cyangwa Miss Universe.
 
Uyu mukobwa yegukanye ikamba atsinze abandi 30 bari bahatanye icyakora 15 nibo babashije kugera mu cyiciro cya nyuma cyanavuyemo Nyampinga.
