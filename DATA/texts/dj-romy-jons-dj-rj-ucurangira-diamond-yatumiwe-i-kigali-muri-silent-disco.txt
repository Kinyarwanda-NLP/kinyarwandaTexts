Romy Jons ni umucuranzi uvangavanga imiziki muri Wasafi by’umwihariko akaba acurangira Diamond, uyu yatumiwe i Kigali mu gitaramo cya Silent Disco kizabera mu muhanda.
 
Romy Jons usibye gucurangira abahanzi, afite album y’indirimbo yagiye ahurizamo abahanzi bakomeye muri Afurika ari nayo iriho indirimbo yakoranye na Meddy irimo kandi Diamond.
 
Usibye iyi, afite izindi yakoranye n’abantu b’ibyamamare nka Morgan Heritage , Vanessa Mdee , Khaligraph Jones , Gnako , Harmonize, Jose Chameleone, Niniola , Ycee, Ray Vanny n’abandi.
 
Romy Jons yatumiwe mu gitaramo cya Silent Disco kibera mu muhanda kimaze kumenyerwa buri tariki 25 Ukuboza cyane ko ari ubwa gatatu kigiye kuba.
 
Muri uyu mwaka iki gitaramo kizabera i Remera ahazwi nko kwa Jules ari naho gisanzwe kibera, aha hakaba hatumiwe abandi bavanga imiziki nka Dj Miller, Dj Danches, Dj Toxxyk, Dj Roger na Vj Mupenzi.
 
Kwinjira muri iki gitaramo bikazaba ari amafaranga 5000frw mu myanya isanzwe n’ibihumbi icumi 10000frw mu myanya y’icyubahiro.
