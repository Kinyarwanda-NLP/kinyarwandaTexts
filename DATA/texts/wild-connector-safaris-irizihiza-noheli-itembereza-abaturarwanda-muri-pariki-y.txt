Sosiyete itembereza ba mukerarugendo  Wild Connector Safaris  igiye gufasha abanyarwanda gutembera ibyiza bitatse u Rwanda biboneka muri Pariki ya y’Akagera muri iyi minsi mikuru isoza umwaka.
 
Sosiyete itembereza ba mukerarugendo  Wild Connector Safaris  igiye gufasha abanyarwanda gutembera ibyiza bitatse u Rwanda biboneka muri Pariki ya y’Akagera muri iyi minsi mikuru isoza umwaka.
 
Uruzinduko ruzaba kuwa Gatandatu tariki 21 Ukuboza 2019, muri Pariki y’Akagera icumbikiye inyamaswa nyinshi.
 
Abazitabira bazabasha kubona amoko atandukanye y’inyamaswa  ziba muri iyo pariki, imwe mu zikomeye muri Afurika nyuma yo kuzanwamo intare n’inkura.
 
Pariki y’akagera irimo n’izindi nyamaswa zitanduknaye nk’impala n’imparage, impongo, inzovu, imvubu, ingwe, inyoni z’amoko asaga 500  n’ibindi.
 
Ushinzwe iyamamazabikorwa muri  Wild Connector Safaris , Rugamba Alexis  yakanguriye cyane cyane ababyeyi kuzana abana bari mu biruhuko, babafasha gusobanukirwa neza ibitatse igihugu cyabo.
 
Ni n’umwanya kandi ku muryango n’abakundana gusoza umwaka bishimye batembera ibyiza bitatse u Rwanda.
 
Ati “Iki gihe abana benshi baba bari mu biruhuko. Ni umwanya mwiza kuri bo wo gutembera no kumenya byinshi ku gihugu cyabo. N’abakuru ni uko, muze dusure pariki yacu y’Akagera tunezezwe no kureba inyamaswa za mbere zikomeye ku isi.”
 
Kujyana na Wild Connector Safaris muri Pariki y’Akagera, umuntu umwe  arasabwa kwishyura amafaranga 30 000 Frw mu gihe abanyamahanga badaturuka mu muryango wa Afurika y’Iburasirazuba bazishyura amadolari 110.
 
Muri ayo mafaranga  harimo itike, uburenganzira bwo kwinjira muri pariki, ifunguro rya ku manywa, abakuyobora n’ibindi.
 
Abantu bakundana baziye rimwe ndetse n’abana bazagabanyirizwaho 10 %.
 
Undi mwihariko w’uru rugendo ni uko hazaba hari umufotozi wabigize umwuga uzafata amafoto kandi buri wese akazahabwa umwanya wo kubaza ibibazo byose ku bijyanye n’ibyiza bitatse u Rwanda.
